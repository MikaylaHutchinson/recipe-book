Jerk Salmon

Description

A deliciously baked jerk salmon recipe that is guranteed to leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Jamaican jerk seasoning, wet and dry
  • Allspice
  • Lawry's seasoned salt
  • Adobo all purpose seasoning

Steps

  1. Season the cuts of salmon to taste
  2. Bake the seasoned salmon at 350° until fully cooked
  3. Remove salmon from the over and enjoy!
