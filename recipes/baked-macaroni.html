Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Description

An oven-baked macaroni and cheese pie. Perfect for sharing as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Shred the cheddar cheese
  2. Boil the macaroni, adding salt to the pasta water
  3. After the macaroni has been boiled to desired consistency, drain the pasta water
  4. Add the paprika and onion powder to the macaroni, to taste
  5. Add the macaroni to a baking pan, with cheese spread throughout
  6. Add a layer of cheese to the top of the macaroni making sure the pasta is fully covered
  7. Bake in the oven at 400° for 30 min or until the top becomes golden with a few brown spots
  8. Remove the pan from the oven and enjoy!
